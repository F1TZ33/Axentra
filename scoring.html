Scoring

Indicative scoring bands

The score creates a structured baseline. It does not replace judgement, but it helps qualify complexity, urgency, and implementation risk in a consistent way.

How to read the result

Lower scores indicate more operating stability. Higher scores suggest greater inconsistency, pressure, or need for structured support.

The goal is not to label the business. The goal is to create clarity around the right starting point.

Score rangeIndicative fit
0–15Foundation / diagnostic-led starting point
16–25Structured / targeted support and system reinforcement
26–35Embedded / deeper involvement and execution support
36–40Strategic Priority / high complexity, urgency, or implementation risk
